Who I am

    Hello, I am Eren Yeager and I am from the island of Paradis. I was born within the Shiganshina district, and that is where I lived until the Titans invaded. The Colossal Titan unleashed waves of Titans which resulted in the loss of my parents and hometown. Ever since that day, I swore that I would wipe out all of the Titans. On my first mission I discovered that I possess the power of the Attack Titan. Ever since then I have used my gift, and will continue to use it until we are free from the Titans oppression.
